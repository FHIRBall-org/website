Healthcare IT Today published an in-depth look at how the **FHIR Business Alliance (FHIRBall)** is driving adoption of FHIR through **real-world business use cases**, not just technical theory.

Full article: [**https://www.healthcareittoday.com/2025/04/28/the-fhir-business-alliance-fhirball-is-focused-on-business-use-cases/**](https://www.healthcareittoday.com/2025/04/28/the-fhir-business-alliance-fhirball-is-focused-on-business-use-cases/)

The piece highlights how FHIRBall brings together a coalition of organizations committed to making interoperability practical, scalable, and aligned with the operational needs of payers, providers, vendors, and innovators across the healthcare ecosystem.

FHIRBall website: [**https://www.fhirball.org/**](https://www.fhirball.org/)
