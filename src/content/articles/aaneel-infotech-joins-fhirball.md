## AaNeel Joins FHIRBall to Help Advance Healthcare Interoperability

We're thrilled to welcome **AaNeel InfoTech** as the newest member of the **FHIR Business Alliance (FHIRBall)** -- a global coalition of organizations committed to accelerating the adoption of the HL7 FHIR standard and building a more connected health ecosystem.

AaNeel published their official announcement here: [**https://www.aaneel.com/aaneel-joins-fhirball/**](https://www.aaneel.com/aaneel-joins-fhirball/)

### Why this matters

FHIRBall brings together companies across the healthcare and health-tech landscape who believe in open, standards-based interoperability and transparent collaboration. You can learn more about the alliance's purpose at: [**https://www.fhirball.org/purpose**](https://www.fhirball.org/purpose)

AaNeel's product suite is built with interoperability at its core, making them a strong addition to the coalition. Their decision to join underscores a shared commitment to enabling seamless data exchange, scalable integrations, and better experiences for patients, providers, and developers.

### What's ahead

As part of FHIRBall, AaNeel will collaborate with fellow members to advance real-world FHIR adoption, contribute to shared best practices, and help expand a thriving, vendor-neutral community focused on solving healthcare's hardest data challenges.

We're excited for the work ahead and grateful to have AaNeel alongside us.
