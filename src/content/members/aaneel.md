AaNeel InfoTech is a healthcare technology company dedicated to advancing interoperability and transforming healthcare delivery through modern, standards-based solutions. Our mission is to simplify clinical and administrative workflows, empower data-driven care, and support the strategic goals of Payers, ACOs, and MSOs through scalable, intelligent technology.

We design and deliver solutions that enable real-time data exchange, patient access, and seamless integration across stakeholders. Our development philosophy emphasizes API-first architecture and modular, future-proof applications that streamline care coordination, enhance provider collaboration, and improve member engagement. From care management platforms to digital member tools, every solution is built with interoperability at its core, ensuring our clients can adapt and thrive in a rapidly evolving healthcare landscape.
