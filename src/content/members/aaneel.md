AaNeel Infotech is a healthcare technology organization focused on advancing secure, standards-based healthcare connectivity through FHIR®-centered architecture. Our mission is to simplify clinical and administrative workflows, strengthen data-driven care coordination, and support the evolving needs of health plans, ACOs, MSOs, IPAs, and provider organizations through scalable, intelligent technology solutions.

Built on an API-first, interoperability-driven foundation, AaNeel delivers real-time healthcare data exchange, longitudinal member access, and seamless connectivity across the healthcare ecosystem. Our platforms are designed to reduce operational friction, improve collaboration across stakeholders, and support modern value-based care initiatives while aligning with CMS interoperability requirements and federal data exchange mandates.

AaNeelCare® is HITRUST certified and developed with a strong commitment to HIPAA-compliant security, privacy, and operational resilience. Our solutions are engineered to support secure healthcare data exchange while maintaining the integrity, protection, and accessibility of sensitive member information across complex healthcare environments.

From interoperable care management platforms to digital member engagement solutions, AaNeel designs modular, future-ready applications that enable organizations to scale efficiently while maintaining flexibility and long-term data accessibility.

**Our Perspective**

We view FHIR® as a catalyst for transforming healthcare data exchange around the member’s longitudinal health journey. Our goal is to help create a connected healthcare ecosystem where information follows the member; analytics are embedded directly into workflows, and secure interoperability becomes a foundational capability rather than a disconnected integration project.
